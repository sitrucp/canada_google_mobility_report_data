Google Community Mobility Report - Canada Provinces & Territory Data

This heatmap visualization shows Google location history change compared to baseline by province and date from Feb 15 to Apr 17. Blue or red colours indicate bigger changes. As COVID-19 pandemic manifested over this time period location visits and duration of stays decreased in Workplace, Retail & Recreation, Transit Stations, Grocery & Pharmacy and Parks locations, and increased in Residential locations.

Data source: Google Community Mobility Report csv format data
